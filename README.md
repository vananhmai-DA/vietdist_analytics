## Power BI Dashboard Insights

The Power BI dashboard is structured into three analytical pages:

1. **Overview**  
   Provides a high-level view of revenue performance, target achievement, profitability, and revenue contribution by region and channel.

2. **Sales Performance**  
   Explains why revenue is under target by analyzing monthly performance, regional gaps, channel contribution, and employee-level performance.

3. **Distributor Analysis**  
   Evaluates fulfillment performance through delivered revenue, quantity fill rate, and on-time delivery rate.

### Key Findings

- VietDist achieved only **54.67% of its revenue target**, while Gross Profit Margin remained stable at around **34.67%**. This suggests that the main issue is revenue scale, not profitability.
- Sales underperformance is concentrated in regions with high targets. **Miền Trung exceeded its target**, while **Miền Nam had the lowest achievement rate despite having the highest target**.
- Traditional Trade is the strongest revenue-contributing channel, while E-commerce requires further investigation before being classified as a weak channel.
- Distributor fulfillment is relatively stable in terms of quantity fill rate, but delivery reliability is weak. The **On-time Delivery Rate is only 40.70%**, indicating a major operational risk.
- Distributors with high revenue contribution but low on-time delivery rate should be prioritized for operational improvement.

### Final Recommendations

- Focus sales recovery efforts on Miền Nam first because it has the largest target gap.
- Use Miền Trung as an internal benchmark for sales execution.
- Use employee ranking for coaching and follow-up, but avoid evaluating employees based only on target achievement percentage.
- Maintain Traditional Trade as the core revenue channel while clarifying the strategic role of E-commerce.
- Improve distributor delivery reliability, especially for distributors with high revenue contribution and low on-time delivery performance.
